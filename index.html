About Us

Just Ship It is a comprehensive delivery management system designed to streamline truck delivery operations for businesses of all sizes. Our platform enables efficient coordination between client companies, dispatch managers, drivers, and administrators.

Key Features:

Delivery Management

Create, track, and manage delivery orders from pending to completion

Driver Management

Hire drivers, track their status, and manage assignments with age verification

Fleet Management

Monitor truck availability, maintenance schedules, and assignments

Real-time Tracking

View live status updates for drivers, trucks, and deliveries

Smart Filtering

Filter deliveries by status, driver, or truck for quick access

Visual Interface

Upload images for drivers and trucks for easy identification

Whether you're managing a small local delivery service or a large logistics operation, Just Ship It provides the tools you need to keep your deliveries moving efficiently and your customers satisfied.
